The Platform

Serious tools. No extraction.

Prosper Platform for Healers is a SaaS bundle — like Google Workspace or Microsoft 365, but purpose-built for the healer community. Every app is designed around one commitment: you own your data, you own your community, and you are never locked in.


Prosper Foundation is the platform provider — a foundation-aligned organization, not an investor-driven company. Our incentives are aligned with yours.

"A home for healers to do their work — without surrendering their practice to a platform that doesn't share their values."

Our Promise

Built on four commitments

01
You own your data

Every piece of content you create, every client list you build, every recording you upload — it belongs to you. Export it all, at any time, in open formats. No hostage-taking.

02
No extraction logic

We are a foundation, not a venture-backed company optimizing for your dependency. We charge for the platform. We don't sell your data, monetize your community, or take a cut of your bookings.

03
Open membership

Any healer, any modality, any tradition. The platform is not gatekept by certification bodies or commercial partners. If you hold space for people to heal, you belong here.

04
Privacy by design

Your clients trust you with sensitive moments. We carry that responsibility seriously — portability, privacy, and product quality are operating constraints, not marketing claims.
